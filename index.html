About Me:

My name is Hal Anderson. I live in South Orange, New Jersey, and I teach flying trapeze in New York City.

When people ask me what I actually do as a trapeze instructor? It's the following:

  • coaching students
  • training staff
  • rigging equipment
  • rig maintenance
  • and the best part of my job, catching people in midair

...but mostly just having fun!

While I still love trapeze, I am looking to branch out and challenge myself in other ways as well. I am looking for several things in my next endeavour.

  1. It has to be something that I enjoy doing.
  2. It should be something that always presenting new challenges.
  3. I'd like work that could possibly be done remote some of the time.
  4. And of course I want decent pay with potential to grow.

When I was in school I always enjoyed my coding classes. I love the challenge of figuring out a soultion to a problem, implementing it, and having it work! I am currently taking a software engineering bootcamp at Thinkful so that I can put that passion for discovery and problem solving to good use. And I think a career as a software engineer can satisfy most if not all of the things I'm looking for.

These days I'm spending a lot of time studying and developing coding skill, and when I'm not doing that you can find me swinging high up in the sky of NYC.
